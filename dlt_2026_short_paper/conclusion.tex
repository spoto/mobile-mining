\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

This paper advocates the use of mining in a mobile device, for blockchains based on
proof of space. It describes our Mokaminter
mining app for Android and shows that it has minimal
CPU, RAM and data exchange costs, which is important in a mobile device.
This shows that mobile mining in a phone is actually feasable.
Mokaminter is meant for blockchains based on the Mokamint engine~\cite{mokamint-source-code} and
the proof of space algorithm in~\cite{Spoto25}. Nevertheless, we think
that it could be adapted to other blockchains based on
proof of space, such as Chia~\cite{AbusalahACKPR17}.

If a proof of space blockchain reaches success, the size of the
disk space for mining would likely become so large that mobile devices would be ruled out.
But a mobile mining app remains a precious tool to start new blockchains
and lead them to the success that then makes such an app outdated.
